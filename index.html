Our Story

Founded in 2015, Sinbad Hair Style has been dedicated to providing premium grooming services to gentlemen who appreciate quality and style. Our team of skilled barbers combines traditional techniques with modern trends to deliver exceptional results.

At Sinbad Hair Style, we believe that a visit to the barbershop should be more than just a haircut—it should be an experience. That's why we've created a relaxed atmosphere where you can unwind while receiving top-notch service.

Every member of our team undergoes rigorous training and stays up-to-date with the latest styles and techniques to ensure you leave looking and feeling your best.
